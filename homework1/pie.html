Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie


One 9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup while suger
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemens,juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 agg yolks,beaten
  • 1(9 inch) pie crust,baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tablespoons white auger

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven: to 350 degrees F(175 degrees c).
  2. Make Lemen Filling: In a medium saucepan ...
    • Whisk together 1 cup suger,flour,cornstarch,and salt.
    • Stir in water,lemen juice and lemen zest.
    • Cook over medium-high heat,stirring frequently,until mixture comes to a boil.
    • Stir i butter.
    • Place agg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot suaerr mixture.
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining suger nixture.
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick.
  3. Make Meringue: In a large glass or metal bowl ...
    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
